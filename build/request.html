Request Gear

Our goal is to gather resources and facilitate redistribution back into the community in an effort to remove the barriers to accessibility. There are undeniable logistical access issues and financial barriers to getting into the outdoors, and oppressive aspects of our society (racism, class and capitalism, cis-heteropatriarchy, trans and queer antagonism, ableism, misogyny and misogynoir, etc) have historically created larger barriers for Black, Indigenous, People of Color, LGBTQIA2S+ and Adaptive folks.

We aim to prioritize distribution to folks who identify as Black, Indigenous, People of Color, LGBTQIA2S+, Adaptive and/or people who have faced barriers in accessing the outdoors.

  1. Look through our inventory here and pick out the gear you would like to request
  2. Fill out our Request form here.
  3. You may request multiple items. If requesting multiple items, please submit a new request if items are held with another person (see ATTN). Please note that some items may have more than one individual requesting, and while we will absolutely do our best to accommodate everyone, a request does not guarantee that you will receive the item.
